The Sanipepla Model

Built within a modified commuter bus, the Sanipepla toilet model is an eco-friendly, self-contained mobile sanitation unit that integrates water recycling technology and waste separation.

  • 💧

    Water Recycling Technology

    Recycles flushing water up to 50 times using advanced filtration, saving up to 9,000 litres per cycle.

  • ♻️

    Resource Recovery

    Separates urine and faecal matter for future conversion into fertilizers or biogas.

  • 🚀

    Scalable Model

    Easy to maintain and adapt for schools, markets, and residential estates using local materials.
